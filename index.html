comic what is it?

This is a webcomic heavily based on the format of the Ms paint adventures by andrew hussie. It doesnt have a premise yet (I dont have anything in mind Im just going with the flow lol)

Anyone can post in my discord channel on the sinecraft discord, what they would like to happen next. You can format the message like:

> Words words words
or
> Do the thing
so that we can tell what is a command and stuff..

Everyone can react to the ones that they want to see be used in the comic. the one with the most people reacting to it (Note: not the most reactions) will be used in the next days comic!

You can react to multiple responses if you like, and you can also post as many responses as you like, its just that only one will be used

There will be a one day period to put your reactions in, and then the next panel will be drawed and uploaded to this site! I think thats all there is to it! : ))
